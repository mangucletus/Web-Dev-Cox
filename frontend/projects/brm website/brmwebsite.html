About BRM

School Projects and Donation

Basic Resilience Mentorship program is a young non-profit organization that operates within the Savannah Region.

Our Aim and Objective

Our main objective is to help deprived and brilliant children (especially young girls) and less privileged young people
to unleash their potentials through mentorship in education and vocational skills development.
We also aimed to create a solution to the SDG1 and SDG4, thus, end poverty and achieve literacy and numeracy, respectively.
